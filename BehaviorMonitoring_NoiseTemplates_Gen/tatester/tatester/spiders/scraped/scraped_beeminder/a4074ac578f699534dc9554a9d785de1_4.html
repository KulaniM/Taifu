Beeminder is a motivational tool that allows you to pledge money to stay on track towards a goal.
If you go off track, we charge you the amount you're pledging on that goal.
Can't use a credit card? You can use PayPal, if you must.

Welcome to Beeminder!

If there is anything you want to do, you know you can do, and yet historically you don't do, then you should beemind it!

Want some help getting started? Check out our quick guide "What do I do first?"

Here's what you need to know:

  1. You control how much you're committing to do, and how big you want your pledge to get.
  2. Each time you go off track (derail), we'll charge you and increase the pledge.
  3. There's a one-week delay to make your goal easier. That includes reducing the pledge, and quitting it altogether.
  4. If something goes wrong, reply to the derail email and we'll always believe you and cancel or reverse the charge.
