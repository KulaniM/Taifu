A mission worth

spreading

At Fitbit, we’re dedicated to helping people lead healthier, more active lives. From tech enthusiasts to triathletes, everyone brings something different to the table. But we all do it for the same reason—to create products and services that make a real difference to real people.

 

We value open minds over egos. We treat coworkers like friends and customers like family. We do the right thing no matter what. And we have a lot of fun doing it.

 

Want to be a part of one of the world’s most loved brands? We’d love to have you.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

The spirit of a startup, the perks of being public

 

We think the best part of Fitbit is the people. But if you’re curious about the other benefits, here’s what it’s like to walk a mile in our shoes.

Get Fit

Wear workout gear on Wednesdays and sweat it out in on-site classes or team challenges.

 

Eat Up

Kitchens are stocked with snacks, breakfast and a beverage lineup that includes a kombucha keg and cold brew coffee.

 

Stay Inspired

Participate in the company Speaker Series, hackathons, personal development workshops & more.

 

Have Fun

From Friday happy hours to holiday parties and monthly All Hands, you’ll find many ways to meet & mingle with coworkers.

 

Be Invested

With equity packages and competitive benefits—including 401(k) matching and an ESPP—everyone has a stake

in Fitbit’s success.

 

Kick Back

Enjoy an unlimited vacation policy, great health insurance options & competitive maternity/paternity plans.
