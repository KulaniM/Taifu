A tool to keep you on track with weight loss or maintenance. It calculates a target zone for how many calories to eat daily and the zone increases as you burn extra calories.

Note: As with any weight program, we advise speaking with a doctor before starting anything new.

Two things make up your Food Plan:

Daily Calorie Estimate

Uses your average activity to give you an estimate of how many calories you should eat to meet your weight goal. This updates throughout the day if you are more or less active than usual. As you log food during the day, the calories you can still eat will update.

Two things make up your Food Plan:

Calories in vs Out

This view is a real-time calculation of your calorie deficit and shows whether you're in the goal zone set by your Food Plan. Your plan's deficit is pro-rated over the entire day.

You will be in your goal zone as long as you are within 50 calories of your deficit goal for the current time of day.

Calories in vs Out

To maintain your weight, you are given a calorie deficit goal of zero. This gauge will show when you are over or under your calorie deficit goal zone. You will be in your zone when you are within 50 calories of your calorie deficit goal.
