Our Mission
We connect athletes to what motivates them and help them find their personal best.
Our ABCs
  1. 01
    Authenticity
    We put the athlete first and build what they will use.
  2. 02
    Balance
    We practice what we preach: there’s always time for a workout. There’s always time for family.
  3. 03
    Craftsmanship
    We strive for excellence through iteration and a determined pursuit of mastery. Perfection is a never-ending journey.
  4. 04
    Camaraderie
    We’re equals, united by common goals. Our teamwork and respect for each other fuels our success.
  5. 05
    Commitment
    We thrive on wholehearted efforts and the spirit of competition. We’re dedicated to our values, our work, our athletes and our team – no halfway, no hesitation.
Founded in 2009 by two friends, Michael Horvath and Mark Gainey, Strava was designed to capture the camaraderie and competition of their days as collegiate rowing teammates. This is a place where athletes can connect and inspire one another with their activities and accomplishments. Now millions strong, Strava connects a global community of athletes finding their personal best, one workout at a time.
