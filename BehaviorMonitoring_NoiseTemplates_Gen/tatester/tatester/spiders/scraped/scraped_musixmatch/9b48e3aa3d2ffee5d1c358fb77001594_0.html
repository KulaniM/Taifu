How to become a Curator

Unlock one of the highest badges

Songsmith, Referee, Tachometer, or Discoverer. By unlocking one of these badges, you'll be able to take the Lyrics Test

Take the Lyrics Test

Transcribe from scratch, check the lyrics and format mistakes. A test composed of 3 questions is the last step towards becoming a Curator

Start your Traineeship

Musixmatch will check your test results. If you pass the test, congratulations! You'll become a Curator, starting from our Trainee level
