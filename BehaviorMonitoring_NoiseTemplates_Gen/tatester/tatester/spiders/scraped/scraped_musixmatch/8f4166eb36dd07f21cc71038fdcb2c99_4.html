How Musixmatch works

Musixmatch is a global community composed of million of users worldwide. Their passion for music is what makes Musixmatch special. Each user can contribute to improving the Musixmatch catalog in a variety of different ways:

Add lyrics

Transcribe lyrics that are missing from our Musixmatch catalog

Sync lyrics

Synchronise lyrics to get them playing in time with the song

Review lyrics

Proofread the lyrics by voting on new lyrics and suggested changes

Translate lyrics

Help others to understand your favorite songs

Tag lyrics

Complete the song info by tagging the song structure

Report lyrics

Found an error in locked lyrics? Create a report and our Content Team will take care of it

Earn points and badges

Inside the Musixmatch community, users can earn points and badges based on what contributions they make to the lyrics.

These points allow users to gain more status and a higher level within the community. Depending on the type of contribution made, users receive different badges. The highest badges give users the privilege to then apply to be a Curator.

Rank up

Users reach different ranks by accumulating points. The user ranking shows the status of the user inside the Musixmatch community. From Newbie to Hero: it'll be a long way to the top!
