Best place to work

Musixmatch was founded in 2010 with the mission to change the way people experience music and lyrics. Musixmatch's team includes over 80 international and talented individuals working on a product reaching millions of music lovers.

BEAUTIFUL BOLOGNA

We are in the very center of Bologna, a city known for its profound history, amazing Italian local food, and also for being Europe's Scientific calculation hub.

flexible working

We care about a balanced lifestyle so our team performs at their best. You are free to adjust your schedule when needed.

come hungry, Get fueled!

Our kitchen is full of free wholesome food and let's not forget our Friday catered lunch where the crew comes together to rewind from a hard working week - we need to fuel our creative energies!

team spirit

We are a young, talented and fun crew, who love solving problems and working harder, better, faster, stronger. Our team spirit is nothing short of awesome!

TECH GEEKS

We love all things technology, thats why we host events focused on AI and Machine Learning at our office, where we get our tech groove on. We invite all tech geeks to join us!

sport sport sport

From cycling to running, functional training, pilates and yoga - we strongly encourage sports, and yes we also offer sport to our crew members.
