What is Narro Sponsorship?

As a Narro Sponsor, you can create advertisements on Narro's articles-to-audio platform. You provide simple ad copy and links/URLs that you want inserted into the beginning of topical Narro readings.

Choose a few keywords or topics by which to find relevant content, and Narro users will be listening to your advertisements inline with their article podcasts.
