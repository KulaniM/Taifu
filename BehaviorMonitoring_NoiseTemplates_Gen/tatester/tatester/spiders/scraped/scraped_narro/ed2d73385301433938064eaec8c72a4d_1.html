Narro Developer APIWorks everywhere you do


Narro creates a personal podcast that you can play on any platform. Here are some recommended players and tips on how to use them with Narro.

Personalize

Narro's flexible API allows anyone to build a way to listen to the web, and a wide array of high-quality voices makes it easier than ever to keep up.

Optimize

Use Narro to automatically generate a podcast from your own blog. Listen to your backlog of readings on the way to work. The possibilities are endless!

Languages

Narro offers automatic detection of your submissions' language. Currently, you can listen to readings on Narro in English, Danish, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Polish, Portugese, Romanian, Russian, Swedish, Turkish, Dutch, and Norwegian.
