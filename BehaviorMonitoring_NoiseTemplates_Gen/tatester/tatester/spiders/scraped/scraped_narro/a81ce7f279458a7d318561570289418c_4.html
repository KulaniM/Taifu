Control Over Your Audio

Your readings will be transcribed to audio in a clear, realistic voice. You can choose from over 25 AI-powered voices with automatic detection of over 10 languages! Narro even extracts audio from video links. Don't worry about ads or formatting - Narro filters out everything but the text.

Listen Wherever You Go

You can listen to the reading immediately. Narro automatically creates a podcast of all your articles, so you can listen on all your devices. Perfect for learning at the gym or daily commute.

Audience Insights for Publishers

Narro Analytics allows you to understand your listening habits and your most valuable publishing websites. For publishers, Narro can automatically generate a subscribable podcast for any website and provide insight into your audience and most popular content.
